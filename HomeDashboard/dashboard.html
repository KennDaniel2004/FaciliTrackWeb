Legal Holiday

Official legal holidays recognized in the Philippines.

Regular Holiday
January 1
New Year's Day
Celebrates the first day of the Gregorian calendar year. A time for new beginnings, resolutions, and family gatherings.
Regular Holiday
April 9
Araw ng Kagitingan
Commemorates the heroism and bravery of Filipino soldiers during World War II, particularly the Battle of Bataan.
Regular Holiday
May 1
Labor Day
Honors the working class and their contributions to society. Workers are given a day of rest and various celebrations are held.
Regular Holiday
June 12
Independence Day
Marks the declaration of Philippine independence from Spain in 1898. Features parades, fireworks, and patriotic celebrations.
Regular Holiday
July 4
Philippine-American Friendship Day
Commemorates the anniversary of the Philippine-American Friendship Treaty and the establishment of diplomatic relations.
Regular Holiday
July 30
Ninoy Aquino Day
Honors Senator Benigno 'Ninoy' Aquino Jr., a key figure in the fight against the Marcos dictatorship who was assassinated in 1983.
Regular Holiday
November 30
Bonifacio Day
Celebrates the birth anniversary of Andres Bonifacio, founder of the Katipunan and a key figure in the Philippine Revolution.
Regular Holiday
December 25
Christmas Day
Celebrates the birth of Jesus Christ. Filipinos observe this with Simbang Gabi, parol lanterns, and family reunions.
Regular Holiday
December 30
Rizal Day
Commemorates the execution of Dr. Jose Rizal, national hero, on December 30, 1896. A day of reflection on nationalism and patriotism.
Special Non-Working
November 1
All Saints' Day
A day to honor deceased loved ones. Families visit cemeteries, clean graves, and hold prayers for the souls of the departed.
Special Non-Working
November 2
All Souls' Day
Commemorates all the faithful departed. Similar to All Saints' Day, families pray for the souls in purgatory.
Special Non-Working
December 26
Christmas Day (Observed)
Observed holiday when Christmas Day falls on a weekend, extending the celebration.
Special Non-Working
December 31
Rizal Day (Observed)
Observed holiday when Rizal Day falls on a weekend, preserving the national hero commemoration.
